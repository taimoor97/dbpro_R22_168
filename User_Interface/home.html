About Us

Our list of awesome services.

Loan Management System is a powerful loan automization software.It helps an organization to manage loan for its employees in an efficient way and providing the employess a facility to apply for loan and get its approval without any difficulty, giving them release from all the manual tasks of loan application process

Loan Policies

The organization can define loan policies and rules to employees according to their rank, providing them with an application to apply for loan

Handle Requests

The organization can receive the employees request for loan and it can approve and disapprove loan according to its terms and conditions.

Payment List

The organzation can update and delete the data of employees w.r.t their paid installents and left installments.

Loan Details

Employees can view Loan policies, loan limit and other details according to their rank and salary.

Loan Application

The employees can apply for loan using an application and get their application to be acccepted or rejected.

Installment Plan

Employees can receive an easy installment plan in case of their acceptance of loan and can get the reports of paid and remaining installments.
